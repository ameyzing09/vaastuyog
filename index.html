Before applying for any home loan, it’s important to determine which Bank specifically suits your requirement best. Among the parameters to consider, INTEREST RATES, LOAN TENURE, LOAN TO VALUE RATIO, and PROCESSING FEE are just our few concerns.

Further, applying for a HOME LOAN requires a specific procedure to be followed. To begin with…, the lender (seller/ builder) will require ORIGINAL PROPERTY DOCUMENTS with all LEGAL and TECHNICAL formalities completed, which you will need to, procure from the lender (seller/ builder).

Once you fill out the application at the bank, an assessment will be carried to ascertain your ABILITY TO REPAY, the ELIGIBLE AMOUNT as well as your CREDIT SCORE i.e CIBIL. Following this, the bank carries out an INDEPENDENT VERIFICATION and VALUATION of the property.

Once all the above mentioned criteria are met, the HOME LOAN will be sanctioned and disbursed.

The basic documents you should be prepared with while going for a Home Loan. Considering the Salaried or the Self Employed the documents varies :

  1. 1 photo (applicant and spouse)
  2. Pan card & Aadhaar card both (applicant and spouse)
  3. Current Residence address proof
  4. Education Certificate (applicant and spouse)
  5. Latest shop Act Licence Xerox (for self-employed)
  6. Income Tax Returns for Last 3 years (for self-employed)
  7. Last 6 months bank statement (for self-employed)
  8. Last 6 months bank Pass Book (for salaried)
  9. Processing fees cheque (Bank from which the ECS will be debited)
  10. Loan account statement if any (applicant and spouse)
